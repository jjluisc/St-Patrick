Reading

Definition

Definition

Participants will read English texts aloud. Judges will evaluate reading comprehension and pronunciation. Those who pass will advance to the next round with preparation time.

Hint

Hint

The texts, selected by the St. Patrick Institute, will be revealed only at the beginning of each round. Competitors may use a monolingual or bilingual dictionary as an aid.

Rounds

Rounds

Round 1: The top 60% based on scores will advance to the next round, each having two 3-minute attempts to read the text.
Round 2: Only the top 30% with the best scores will make it to the next round.
Round 3: The top three scores will win.

Standars

Standars

1. Starters: Syllabic reading
2. Elementary: Word-by-word reading
3. Basic: Phrased reading
4. Middle: Fluent reading

The criteria to be assessed are: Volume, articulation, fluency, and rhythm.
